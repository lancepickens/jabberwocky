## Key Observations

1. **Magnetic confinement (tokamak) Q has been stalled since 1997**: JET's Q=0.67 from actual D-T fuel remains the record after nearly 30 years.
2. **ICF (NIF) has shown rapid Q improvement since 2022**: From Q=1.54 to Q=4.13 in less than 3 years.
3. **No device has achieved engineering breakeven**: NIF's Q=4.13 uses ~300-400 MJ of wall-plug electricity for 8.6 MJ of fusion output (Q_eng ~0.02-0.03).
4. **Extrapolated vs actual Q**: JT-60's Q=1.25 was extrapolated from D-D results, not actual D-T fusion.

## NIF Future Projections
- At 2.6 MJ laser capacity (est. 2025-2027): Could achieve Q = 5-8x, yielding 10-20 MJ
- At 3 MJ laser capacity (est. ~2030): Could achieve Q > 10x, yielding 30+ MJ
