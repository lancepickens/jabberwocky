# Quantum Computing: Real-World Applications (2025-2026)

## Market Overview

- Global quantum computing market: USD 1.8-3.5 billion in 2025, projected USD 5.3 billion by 2029 (CAGR 32.7%).
- Currently in NISQ (Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum) era -- transitional phase.
- Hybrid quantum-classical approaches dominate near-term commercial strategy.

## Drug Discovery & Life Sciences

Drug discovery is one of the most promising near-term applications, addressing the decade-long, multi-billion-dollar challenge of bringing treatments from concept to clinic.

### Key Developments
- **Roche (late 2025):** Quantum-powered molecular simulation identified three promising Alzheimer's drug candidates in 18 months instead of the typical 4-6 years.
- **IonQ (Oct 2025):** Claimed quantum advantage in drug discovery, surpassing classical methods in chemistry simulations.
- **St. Jude / University of Toronto:** First time quantum computing was successfully used for a drug discovery project with experimental validation (published in Nature Biotechnology).
- **Boehringer Ingelheim + PsiQuantum:** Exploring electronic structures of metalloenzymes critical for drug metabolism.
- **AstraZeneca + AWS/IonQ/NVIDIA:** Demonstrated quantum-accelerated computational chemistry workflow.
- **Amgen + Quantinuum:** Studying peptide binding with quantum capabilities.
- **IBM + Moderna:** Simulated mRNA sequences using hybrid quantum-classical approach.
- **Pasqal:** First quantum algorithm for protein hydration analysis on neutral-atom hardware.

## Finance & Optimization

Finance is emerging as one of the first industries with measurable quantum computing potential.

### Portfolio Optimization
- **HSBC + IBM:** Quantum-enhanced models improved predictions in corporate bond trading.
- **Vanguard + IBM:** Quantum-classical hybrid workflows for portfolio construction matching classical methods.
- **Goldman Sachs & JPMorgan:** Quantum algorithms for portfolio optimization and risk analysis, modeling thousands of correlated market variables.

### Risk Management & Fraud Detection
- **Intesa Sanpaolo:** Quantum machine learning classifiers outperformed traditional fraud detection methods.
- **D-Wave Application:** One bank completed analysis in 7 seconds that would classically take years.
- **Goldman Sachs (2025):** Risk analysis processing speeds up to 25x faster than classical models.

### Finance Market Share
- Finance sector holds ~20% of total quantum computing applications.

## Logistics & Supply Chain
- IBM partnered with a commercial vehicle manufacturer to optimize deliveries across 1,200 NYC locations.
- Quantum computing identifies most efficient delivery routes, reducing travel time and fuel consumption.

## Climate & Materials Science
- Quantum computers enable climate simulations at finer spatial scales.
- Partnerships between quantum companies and meteorological agencies show promise for regional weather prediction.

## Cybersecurity
- Quantum computing threatens current encryption (RSA, ECC) but also enables quantum-resistant cryptography.
- Post-quantum cryptography standards are being deployed proactively.

## Realistic Assessment
- Most enterprises still depend on classical computing for day-to-day operations.
- Hybrid quantum-classical approaches provide the most near-term value.
- Practical enterprise quantum computing likely to emerge in the early 2030s.
- Firms experimenting now build expertise and infrastructure advantages.
